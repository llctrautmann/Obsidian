# Dot Product
The scalar Vector-Vector product is the dot product [^1]. 

$$\large\tag{1}
\boldsymbol{x}^{\top} \boldsymbol{y}=\sum_{i=1}^n x_i y_i
$$

The opposite $\mathbf{x}\mathbf{y}^\top$ is called the [[outer product]] [^2].
